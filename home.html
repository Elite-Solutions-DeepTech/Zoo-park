The Vizag Zoo, formally known as Indira Gandhi Zoological Park, is located in Visakhapatnam, India. The zoo is renowned for its naturalistic enclosures and conservation efforts, making it a popular destination for both locals and tourists. It also offers recreational activities like boating and a train ride, making it an enjoyable experience for visitors of all ages.
